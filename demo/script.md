# Scout Demo Video Script (~3 minutes)

## Opening (0:00 - 0:25)
**Visual**: Dark screen, text fades in

**Narration**:
"Before every sales call, you spend thirty minutes browsing five different websites. Company website. LinkedIn. Crunchbase. Google News. Job boards. Copying and pasting into notes. By the time you're done, your meeting is about to start."

**Visual**: Quick montage of browser tabs, manual copying

## The Solution (0:25 - 0:45)
**Visual**: Scout dashboard appears

**Narration**:
"Scout does all of this in two minutes. One search. Real websites. Real-time data. A complete briefing ready before you finish your coffee."

**Visual**: Zoom into search bar, type "Stripe"

## Live Demo (0:45 - 2:15)
**Visual**: Click "Research" button

**Narration**:
"Watch what happens. Scout's AI agents are now navigating real websites. Not pulling from stale training data. Actually visiting stripe.com, LinkedIn, Crunchbase, Google News, and their careers page."

**Visual**: Show progress tracker with each step completing

(0:55) "First, it visits the company website. Extracting products, team members, and technology details."

(1:05) "Then Google News for the latest headlines. Funding announcements, product launches, partnerships."

(1:15) "LinkedIn gives us company size, key decision makers, and industry focus."

(1:25) "Crunchbase reveals funding history, investors, and growth stage."

(1:35) "And job listings tell us exactly what technologies they use and where they're growing."

**Visual**: Briefing appears

(1:45) "In under two minutes, Scout delivers a complete briefing."

## Briefing Walkthrough (2:15 - 2:45)
**Visual**: Scroll through briefing sections

**Narration**:
"Key people with titles. Recent news summarized. Their tech stack confirmed from job listings. Growth signals: they're hiring a hundred and twenty engineers. And most importantly, conversation starters tailored to what we found."

**Visual**: Highlight talking points section

"These aren't generic icebreakers. They reference Stripe's AI fraud detection launch, their Africa expansion, and their Anthropic partnership. Real talking points from real data."

## Close (2:45 - 3:00)
**Visual**: Scout logo and tagline

**Narration**:
"Scout. AI-powered company research in seconds. Built with Amazon Nova Act for live website navigation and Nova Two Lite for intelligent synthesis."

**Visual**: #AmazonNova hashtag, GitHub link

---

## Recording Plan
1. Use Playwright to script the walkthrough (controlled timing)
2. Narrate with edge-tts (professional voice, en-US-GuyNeural)
3. Edit with ffmpeg (text overlays, transitions)
4. Target: 2:50 - 3:00 runtime
5. Resolution: 1920x1080

## Voice Notes
- Pace: moderate, confident, not rushed
- No filler words
- Emphasize "real websites, real-time data" vs "stale training data"
- Emphasize time savings: "30 minutes to 2 minutes"
